System Notes

  • Solid buttons instead of transparent ones, so they hit like neon bricks.
  • Matrix rain can be toggled, sped up, slowed down, or blasted into burst mode.
  • Accent color cycles through several modes to keep the portal looking lively.
  • Sound is generated locally in the browser with tiny synth beeps.
  • Fullscreen, copy-link tools, glitch mode, random titles, and a help modal are built in.
Tip: press G for glitch, R for rain, S for sound, F for fullscreen, H for help.
